# Green Tree Token and Market Policy
## Version 2.0.0

## 1. Token Status

GTREE is a Classic SPL Token on Solana Mainnet with 9 decimals and a fixed maximum supply of 1,000,000,000 GTREE.

At launch:

- GTREE is freely transferable;
- no buyer lock applies;
- no vesting applies;
- no staged release applies;
- no claim period applies;
- ordinary holders may transfer, buy, sell, and swap GTREE;
- Mint Authority is revoked after complete mint and allocation verification.

## 2. Market Price

The public DEX market determines the GTREE price. Green Tree does not publish a separate guaranteed internal price.

The official website must use a live market-derived quote from the supported GTREE liquidity route. The website must disclose estimated output, price impact, slippage, network fee, and quote expiration before the user signs.

## 3. Website Transactions

The website is an interface, not a private exchange-rate authority. A website purchase must use the same public market mechanism available through supported Solana wallets and DEX interfaces.

## 4. Market Risk

Price can rise or fall. Market depth can change. A displayed quote is not guaranteed after expiration. Users must review the final wallet transaction before signing.
