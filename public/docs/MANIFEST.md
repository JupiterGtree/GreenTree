# Green Tree Manifest
## Version 2.0.0

> **Grow Together.**

Green Tree begins with a simple belief: technology has value only when people use it with purpose.

We choose an open market because ownership should be clear. GTREE is not built around hidden prices, private transfer advantages, or restrictions that ordinary holders cannot see. From launch, the token belongs to the people who hold it. They may transfer it, trade it, swap it, or keep it.

We choose transparency because trust cannot grow in ambiguity. Material treasury actions, liquidity additions, environmental mission budgets, and verified outcomes should be visible through public records.

We choose nature as a responsibility, not a costume. Green Tree intends to carry out more than ten substantial environmental missions each year. A mission is meaningful only when its location, executor, budget, evidence, and result can be examined.

We choose long-term work over temporary noise. Price may rise or fall. Attention may arrive and disappear. The value of Green Tree must also be measured by the strength of its community, the quality of its systems, and the environmental work it can prove.

A forest is not built by a slogan. It grows through roots, cooperation, care, and time.
