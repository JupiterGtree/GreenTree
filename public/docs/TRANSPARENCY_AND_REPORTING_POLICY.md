# Green Tree Transparency and Reporting Policy
## Version 2.0.0

Green Tree should publish material records for:

- token identity and authorities;
- Mint Authority revocation;
- treasury-controlled addresses;
- allocation balances and material movements;
- liquidity threshold calculations and transactions;
- environmental mission budgets, payments, evidence, and outcomes;
- material policy changes;
- security incidents affecting users or treasury assets.

The official record belongs on https://gtree.land.

Transparency does not require the publication of private keys, seed phrases, internal security details, confidential personal information, or legally restricted material.
